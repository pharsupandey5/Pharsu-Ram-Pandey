Legal Services

Criminal Defense & Litigation

Criminal defense and litigation services encompass representing individuals accused of crimes, from minor offenses to serious felonies, ensuring their rights are protected throughout investigations, trials, and appeals. This includes developing defense strategies, negotiating plea deals, and advocating in court to achieve the best possible outcomes.

Corporate Compliance: Registration, Update & Termination

Corporate compliance services involve assisting businesses with company registration, ongoing compliance with legal and regulatory requirements, updates to corporate structures, and proper termination or dissolution processes to ensure smooth operations and avoid legal pitfalls.

Family & Property Law: Court Marriage, Divorce & Partition

Family and property law services cover matters such as court marriages, divorce proceedings, child custody, alimony, and property partition, providing compassionate and effective legal support to resolve familial disputes and secure equitable distributions.

Constitutional & Public Law

Constitutional and public law services focus on challenging government actions, protecting fundamental rights, interpreting constitutional provisions, and handling public interest litigations to uphold the rule of law and democratic principles.

Intellectual Property Law

Intellectual property law services include protecting inventions, trademarks, copyrights, patents, and trade secrets through registration, enforcement against infringement, and advisory on IP management to safeguard creative and innovative assets.

Foreign Direct Investment

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) services assist foreign investors and local entities in navigating Nepal's investment laws, preparing and submitting investment proposals, obtaining necessary approvals from the Department of Industry and Investment Board, structuring joint ventures, ensuring compliance with repatriation rules, and addressing sector-specific regulations to facilitate secure and successful cross-border investments.
